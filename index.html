How VEIL Works
01
Encrypt client-side. Your collateral amount is encrypted in your browser using Zama's Relayer SDK before being sent onchain.
02
Compute on ciphertext. The contract calculates your health factor using FHE operations — no decryption ever happens onchain.
03
Bots are blind. Liquidators cannot read your health factor. They must attempt blindly — if you're healthy, the tx reverts.
04
Only you decrypt. Using your wallet signature, you can decrypt your own position via the KMS — no one else can.
